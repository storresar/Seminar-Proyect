PROCESS DESCRIPTION

To understand the problem what we used was contextual research, because we used the observation of the situation and we also used the experience that the members of the group had regarding the problem, that is where the context of this problem arises. After really understanding the problem, it allowed us to realize what the causes of the problem were and what the consequences were.


In decision with the team it was decided to use the Universal Design, this because it was assumed that the design of the application should be presented so that it can be used by the great diversity of the type of people, this will produce that the amount of users that will use the application will be maximized, also we used the applications that were similar of transportation services since these already had a great experience in the design for the user, and we took the best characteristics of each one for the design of ours. We also took into account all the information data inputs that a program like this needed, which was the driver's information, the information taken by the carpooling service as can be visualized in the prototype.
