CONTEXT

One of the major problems currently presented in the city of Bogota after the COVID-19 pandemic is the issue of transportation, which includes or derives from other issues such as safety and the quality of the transportation service that students decide to take (whether bus, cab, Uber, etc.). It is known that mobility in the city of Bogota before the pandemic was complicated, to such an extent that people sought other options for quick transportation. However, during the COVID-19 pandemic, mobility in Bogota improved considerably, even reducing CO2 emissions into the atmosphere from vehicles as jobs and classes (both universities and schools) were handled virtually or semi-presential. After the pandemic, mobility returned as it was before but now bringing many problems such as the issue of security or the same "trancones" that can cause uncertainty, fear or even annoyance and dissatisfaction with the Government of Bogota (reference to the mayor of Bogota). One of the most critical points is the issue of security, since it is true that there has been a decrease in the tickling of public transportation services, but now there are new forms of robbery such as massive thefts with bladed weapons or firearms, scopolamine and others. Now, the students of the Universidad El Bosque have realized the risks that may exist at the time of transportation and have decided to create groups on Whatsapp from different locations to transport among several classmates where a student proposes transportation either their own or ask for a service like beat or uber for transportation, but still remains unsafe because anyone with the link of the Whatsapp group can enter and impersonate a student of the university.

This is why it is required to make a mobile application for students of the Universidad El Bosque especially for the modality mostly known as Wheels (maintaining the service and operation that is being handled in the Whatsapp groups created), within it we want to ensure the safety and integrity of the students to be able to transport to the Universidad El Bosque or to their homes at any time. The idea of the application is to publish a service offered by a student of the University where it says the route where it will pass and at what time of class will arrive or at what time it leaves the university, so that any other user can request the service and others, it should be noted that the service is not free but it will not be so expensive so that it is easily accessible to all students, replacing services such as:


  • Cabs
  • Uber
  • Beat
  • Didi
  • Sitp
  • Transmilenio
